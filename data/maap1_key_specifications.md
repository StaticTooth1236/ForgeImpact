## 1. SCOPE AND PURPOSE

This Key Specifications Document defines the critical performance, weight, propulsion, and operational specifications for the Eurus Systems MAAP-1 "AetherForge" tandem-rotor heavy-lift helicopter. All specifications herein are derived from and subordinate to the Program Requirements Baseline (PRB) document AF-MAAP1-PRB-0001, Rev A.

This document presents both **Threshold** (contractually binding minimum) and **Objective** (design goal) values. All specifications apply to the baseline "Green Aircraft" configuration unless explicitly noted as variant-specific.

### 1.1 Document Organization

- **Section 2:** General Characteristics
- **Section 3:** Weight and Payload Specifications
- **Section 4:** Performance Specifications (Speed, Range, Endurance)
- **Section 5:** Hover and Altitude Performance
- **Section 6:** Rotor System and Propulsion Specifications
- **Section 7:** Fuel System and Consumption
- **Section 8:** Flight Envelope and Operational Limits
- **Section 9:** Autonomy and Mission Systems
- **Section 10:** Reliability and Maintainability
