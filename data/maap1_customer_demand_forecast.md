**Customer Demand Forecast Document: Eurus Systems MAAP-1 Program**

**Executive Summary**

The Eurus Systems MAAP-1 program is a cutting-edge aerospace development project that provides advanced aerial monitoring and awareness platforms for various customer segments. This document outlines the expected demand drivers, key requirements, and production implications for each customer segment, providing a comprehensive understanding of the program's demand forecast.

**Customer Segments**

### 1. Civilian / Wildfire Response Agencies

* **Expected Demand Drivers:**
	+ Wildfire seasons and event frequencies
	+ Growth of state and federal agencies' wildfire management budgets
	+ Increasing demand for aerial monitoring and suppression capabilities
* **Impact on Integrated Master Schedule and Production Ramp:**
	+ Peak demand periods during wildfire seasons will require accelerated production and delivery schedules
	+ Increased demand may lead to a ramp-up of production from Q2 to Q4 of each year
* **Key Requirements or Constraints:**
	+ Compliance with Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) regulations
	+ Integration with existing agency systems and infrastructure
	+ High priority on system reliability and uptime during critical wildfire events
* **Interdependencies and Risk:**
	+ Surge in wildfire demand may impact military delivery timelines, requiring adjustments to the Integrated Master Schedule
	+ Potential for concurrent production of military and civilian systems, requiring careful resource allocation

### 2. Military / Defense Customers

* **Expected Demand Drivers:**
	+ Logistics and supply chain modernization initiatives
	+ Growing demand for Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR) capabilities
	+ Increasing need for advanced aerial monitoring platforms in support of military operations
* **Impact on Integrated Master Schedule and Production Ramp:**
	+ Military customers typically have longer lead times and require more advanced planning
	+ Production may be influenced by the military's fiscal year budget cycles (Q1 to Q3)
* **Key Requirements or Constraints:**
	+ Compliance with Department of Defense (DoD) regulations and standards
	+ Integration with existing military systems and infrastructure
	+ High priority on system security and encryption
* **Interdependencies and Risk:**
	+ Changes in military demand may impact civilian delivery timelines, requiring adjustments to the Integrated Master Schedule
	+ Potential for concurrent production of military and civilian systems, requiring careful resource allocation

### 3. Potential International Customers

* **Expected Demand Drivers:**
	+ Growing demand for aerial monitoring and awareness platforms in support of disaster response and natural resource management
	+ Increasing interest in MAAP-1 technology from international partners and allies
	+ Opportunities for export and international sales
* **Impact on Integrated Master Schedule and Production Ramp:**
	+ International sales may require additional planning and coordination with foreign governments and partners
	+ Production may be influenced by international market conditions and regulatory requirements
* **Key Requirements or Constraints:**
	+ Compliance with international regulations and standards
	+ Integration with existing international systems and infrastructure
	+ High priority on system adaptability and flexibility for international deployment
* **Interdependencies and Risk:**
	+ Changes in international demand may impact military and civilian delivery timelines, requiring adjustments to the Integrated Master Schedule
	+ Potential for concurrent production of international and domestic systems, requiring careful resource allocation

**Program Implications**

* Changes in one customer segment may impact the overall program, particularly in terms of production ramp and delivery timelines.
* The program must be flexible and adaptable to respond to changes in demand from each customer segment.
* Careful resource allocation and production planning are essential to meet the diverse needs of each customer segment.
* The Integrated Master Schedule and Manufacturing Ramp Plan must be regularly reviewed and updated to reflect changes in demand and program requirements.

**Recommendations**

* Develop a comprehensive understanding of the demand drivers and requirements for each customer segment.
* Establish a flexible production planning process that can respond to changes in demand.
* Conduct regular reviews of the Integrated Master Schedule and Manufacturing Ramp Plan to ensure alignment
