**Safety Requirements Document**

**Document ID:** SAF-001
**Program:** Eurus Systems MAAP-1
**Date:** June 20, 2023

**Introduction:**

The Eurus Systems MAAP-1 program aims to design, develop, and deploy a microwave atmospheric absorption platform that provides high-resolution, real-time atmospheric sensing and communication capabilities. The safety of the platform, its users, and the surrounding environment is of utmost importance. This Safety Requirements Document outlines the safety requirements for the MAAP-1 platform, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards and adherence to industry best practices.

**Safety Requirements:**

1. **Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) and Radio-Frequency Interference (RFI)**
	* The MAAP-1 platform must comply with FCC Part 15 and IEC 61000-3-2 standards for EMI and RFI mitigation.
	* The platform must be designed to minimize electromagnetic emissions and ensure safe operation in the 24 GHz frequency band.
2. **Cyber Security**
	* The MAAP-1 platform must comply with NIST SP 800-53 and ISO 27001 standards for cyber security.
	* The platform must have robust security features, including secure communication protocols, access controls, and encryption.
3. **Mechanical and Structural Safety**
	* The MAAP-1 platform must be designed to withstand various environmental conditions, including extreme temperatures, wind, and precipitation.
	* The platform must meet safety standards for mechanical and structural integrity, including those related to rotor blades, hubs, and other critical components.
4. **Firefighting and Emergency Response**
	* The Firefighting variant must be designed to meet standards for firefighting and emergency response, including those related to water tank capacity, nozzle design, and control systems.
	* The platform must have a firefighting control system that ensures safe and effective operation.
5. **Electrical and Hydraulic Safety**
	* The MAAP-1 platform must comply with regulatory standards for electrical and hydraulic safety, including those related to power distribution, wiring, and hydraulic systems.
	* The platform must have robust safety features, including ground fault protection, overcurrent protection, and hydraulic pressure relief systems.
6. **User Safety and Training**
	* The MAAP-1 platform must be designed to ensure user safety, including those related to operation, maintenance, and repair.
	* The program must provide comprehensive training and documentation for users, operators, and maintenance personnel.
7. **Environmental and Regulatory Compliance**
	* The MAAP-1 platform must comply with regulatory standards for environmental impact, including those related to noise, emissions, and waste management.
	* The platform must be designed to minimize environmental impact and ensure safe operation in various environments.

**Certification and Compliance:**

The MAAP-1 platform must be certified by a recognized third-party testing laboratory to ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards. The certification process will include testing and evaluation of the platform's safety features, performance, and environmental impact.

**Manufacturing and Testing:**

The MAAP-1 platform must be manufactured and tested in accordance with the Manufacturing Ramp Plan (MRRP) and industry best practices. The testing process will include evaluation of the platform's safety features, performance, and environmental impact.
