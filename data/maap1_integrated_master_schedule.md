## 1. INTRODUCTION

### 1.1 Purpose

This Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) provides the comprehensive timeline for the Eurus Systems MAAP-1 (Modular Autonomous Aerospace Platform - Generation 1) program from program initiation through Initial Operational Capability (IOC) and Full-Rate Production (FRP). This schedule integrates all major program activities including engineering design, manufacturing development, testing, qualification, production ramp-up, and operational deployment.

### 1.2 Scope

The IMS covers:
- All engineering development phases (PDR through CDR)
- Manufacturing readiness and tooling development
- Ground and flight test programs
- Production ramp from LRIP through FRP
- Delivery schedules aligned with customer demand
- Support equipment and training system development

### 1.3 Schedule Baseline

This schedule represents the **Program Management Baseline** as of January 2025. All schedule variances will be tracked against this baseline through formal change control processes.

### 1.4 Schedule Management Approach

- **Critical Path Method (CPM)** utilized for schedule network analysis
- **Bi-weekly** schedule updates and variance analysis
- **Monthly** Integrated Baseline Reviews (IBR) with stakeholders
- **Quarterly** comprehensive schedule risk assessments
- Schedule margin maintained at key milestones (10-15% reserve)

## 2. PROGRAM OVERVIEW

### 2.1 Program Objectives

The MAAP-1 program will deliver a next-generation modular aerospace platform with the following key characteristics:

- **Modularity:** Configurable payload and mission systems
- **Autonomy:** Advanced AI-driven flight control systems
- **Performance:** Mach 2.5+ capability with extended range
- **Production:** Scalable manufacturing to 180+ units over program life

### 2.2 Program Duration

- **Program Start:** January 2025
- **First Flight:** Q2 2028
- **Initial Operational Capability (IOC):** Q1 2030
- **Full-Rate Production (FRP):** Q3 2030
- **Program Completion:** Q4 2035

**Total Program Duration:** 132 months (11 years)

### 2.3 Production Quantities

Per Program Requirements Baseline:

| Phase | Quantity | Purpose |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Development Test Articles | 3 | Ground & flight testing |
| Flight Test Articles | 6 | Flight qualification |
| LRIP Units | 18 | Initial production & operational test |
| FRP Units (Lot 1-3) | 60 | Initial operational deployment |
| FRP Units (Lot 4-6) | 60 | Sustained operations |
| FRP Units (Lot 7-9) | 45 | Final production lots |
| **Total Production** | **192** | **Full program delivery** |
